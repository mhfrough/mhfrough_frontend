This Privacy Policy (the “Policy”) explains how Mohammad Hamza, an individual independent developer operating mhfrough.dev (the “Provider”, “I”, “me”, or “my”), collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards information when you (“you” or the “User”) access the website located at mhfrough.dev (the “Site”). If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the Site.

1. Information I Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Contact form – name, email address, phone number (optional), and message.
  • Feedback form – your feedback text and optional contact details.
  • Blog comments – your name and comment content.
  • Chat – messages sent through the live-chat widget.
  • Push notifications – a device token (via Firebase Cloud Messaging) if you opt in.

Information collected automatically

When you visit the Site, my server and client-side code automatically record anonymous session data to help me understand how the Site is used:

  • Pages visited – the URL paths you navigate to during your visit.
  • Time on page – how long you spend on each page.
  • Referrer URL – the page or search engine that brought you here (if your browser sends one).
  • Screen resolution – your device’s screen dimensions.
  • Browser language – your browser’s preferred language setting.
  • Approximate location – a coarse, city-level estimate derived from network information for security and analytics purposes; this is not precise GPS location.
  • Interaction events – named actions such as button clicks or form submissions, recorded to understand which features are used.
  • Session identifier – a randomly generated ID stored in your browser’s sessionStorage to link the above data points within a single visit. It is not persisted across browser sessions and is not linked to your identity unless you start a chat.

I do not collect IP addresses beyond what the web server logs transiently, nor do I collect payment-card details, government identity numbers, or other special-category sensitive personal data through the Site.

I process your information only where I have a lawful basis to do so, namely: (a) your consent (for example, when you submit a form or opt in to notifications); (b) the performance of, or steps taken at your request prior to, a contract or Engagement; (c) my legitimate interests in operating, securing, and improving the Site, provided these are not overridden by your rights; and (d) compliance with a legal obligation. 3. Cookies & Local Storage

This Site uses:

  • Session cookies – for authentication when the administrator accesses the admin area.
  • sessionStorage – to store your anonymous visitor session ID for the duration of your browser session (cleared when you close the tab or browser).
  • localStorage – to remember your consent preference and to prefill forms with information you previously entered.

No third-party advertising or cross-site tracking cookies are used.

  • To respond to your inquiry, project request, or message.
  • To prepare quotations, invoices, and deliverables in connection with an Engagement you request.

  • To detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, security incidents, and technical issues.

I do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. AI-Assisted Chat Replies

The live-chat feature may use an AI language model (powered by Google Gemini) to generate automatic replies when I am not immediately available. If AI auto-reply is active:

  • Your chat messages and the conversation history are sent to Google’s Gemini API to generate a response.
  • Google’s handling of data for the Gemini API is governed by the Google Privacy Policy and the applicable API terms of service.
  • I do not use your chat content to train AI models.
  • AI-generated replies are automated responses and may be inaccurate; I review conversations and may follow up personally. Please do not submit confidential or sensitive information through the chat.

6. Third-Party Services & Sub-Processors

This Site relies on the following third-party service providers to operate. Each receives only the data needed to perform its function and processes it under its own terms and privacy policy:

  • Render – the cloud platform that hosts the Site and its database. Standard server logs may be processed transiently for security and reliability.
  • Supabase Storage – storage and delivery of media files (such as images) used on the Site.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) by Google – web push notifications. Governed by the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google Gemini API – AI-assisted chat replies (see Section 5 above).
  • Resend – delivery of email notifications and replies related to your inquiry; your name, email, and message are processed to send these emails.

No third-party advertising networks or cross-site tracking services are active on this Site. I am not responsible for the independent data-handling practices of these third parties; please review their respective policies.
