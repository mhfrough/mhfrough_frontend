Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Mohammad Hamza (“I”, “me”, or “my”), operating mhfrough.dev, collects, uses, and protects information when you visit this website. By using this site you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information I Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Contact form – name, email address, phone number (optional), and message.
  • Feedback form – your feedback text and optional contact details.
  • Blog comments – your name and comment content.
  • Chat – messages sent through the live-chat widget.
  • Push notifications – a device token (via Firebase Cloud Messaging) if you opt in.

Information collected automatically

When you visit this site, my server and client-side code automatically record anonymous session data to help me understand how the site is used:

  • Pages visited – the URL paths you navigate to during your visit.
  • Time on page – how long you spend on each page.
  • Referrer URL – the page or search engine that brought you here (if your browser sends one).
  • Screen resolution – your device’s screen dimensions.
  • Browser language – your browser’s preferred language setting.
  • Interaction events – named actions such as button clicks or form submissions, recorded to understand which features are used.
  • Session identifier – a randomly generated ID stored in your browser’s sessionStorage to link the above data points within a single visit. It is not persisted across browser sessions and is not linked to your identity unless you start a chat.

I do not collect IP addresses beyond what the web server logs transiently, nor do I collect payment information, government IDs, or other sensitive personal data.

2. Cookies & Local Storage

This site uses:

  • Session cookies – for authentication when you access the admin area.
  • sessionStorage – to store your anonymous visitor session ID for the duration of your browser session (cleared when you close the tab or browser).
  • localStorage – to remember your consent preference and prefill forms with information you previously entered.

No third-party advertising or tracking cookies are used.

3. How I Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiry, project request, or message.
  • To send push notifications if you have opted in (you can revoke this at any time in your browser settings).
  • To understand how visitors use this site so I can improve content and features.
  • To monitor which pages and interactions are most useful.

I do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. AI-Assisted Chat Replies

The live-chat feature on this site may use an AI language model (powered by Google Gemini) to generate automatic replies when I am not immediately available. If AI auto-reply is active:

  • Your chat messages and the conversation history are sent to Google’s Gemini API to generate a response.
  • Google’s data handling for the Gemini API is governed by the Google Privacy Policy and the applicable API terms of service.
  • I do not use your chat content to train AI models.
  • AI-generated replies are clearly automatic responses; I review conversations and may follow up personally.

5. Third-Party Services

This site integrates the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) by Google – for push notifications. Governed by the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google Gemini API – for AI-assisted chat replies (see section 4 above).

No other third-party analytics or tracking services are active on this site.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained only as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected (e.g., to respond to your inquiry) or as required for legitimate business purposes. Anonymous visitor session data is retained for a limited period to analyse site usage trends and is then deleted. You may request deletion of any personal data at any time.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data I hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for push notifications via your browser settings.
  • Object to the processing of your data for site analytics purposes.

To exercise any of these rights, email me at mhfrough@yahoo.com.

8. Data Security

I take reasonable technical measures to protect your data. However, no transmission over the internet is 100% secure. I cannot guarantee absolute security but will notify you if a breach affects your data.

9. Children’s Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. I do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has submitted data, please contact me and I will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

I may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
