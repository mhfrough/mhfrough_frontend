18

Accessibility

  • Text meets WCAG AA contrast on every surface (bone on charcoal ≈ 11:1).
  • Every interactive element keeps a visible focus ring.
  • Touch targets are at least 44 × 44px.
  • Semantic HTML and ARIA labels on icon-only controls.
  • prefers-reduced-motion is honoured for transitions and reveals.
  • Colour never carries meaning alone — pair it with text or an icon.

The golden rule

Every visual property comes from a design token.
